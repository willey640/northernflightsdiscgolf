Barlo

Bio

  • Name / Alias: Barlo
  • Date of Birth: July 2, 1984
  • Place of Origin: The Swamp Lands, Big Island
  • Species: Sasquatch
  • Height: 7 ft
  • Weight: 400 lbs
  • Hobbies: Reading, bird watching, disc golf
  • Disc Golf Strengths: Raw distance, powerful drives
  • Disc Golf Weaknesses: Short putts, tight wooded fairways
  • Favorite Disc: Overstable distance drivers
  • Special Skill: Effortless max-distance throws
  • Personality: Friendly, well-spoken, intelligent
  • Physical Description: Towering, broad-shouldered Sasquatch with a calm presence
  • Connection to NFDG: Commentary and news desk personality

Backstory

Barlo first appeared as a mascot for a paintball team known as the Swamp Apes, haunting the old course down Riemer’s Road where he enjoyed startling people parked along the roadside. During his long migration toward British Columbia, Barlo stumbled upon a group of disc golfers and became fascinated by the flight of the discs.

At first, Barlo unknowingly collected any discs he found, unaware that the names written on them meant they belonged to someone. Over time, he stepped out of the shadows and met disc golfers who didn’t fear or judge him—they were simply happy to welcome another player to the course.

As he continued his migration, Barlo played courses across the region, made friends, returned countless lost discs, and eventually found a true home with Northern Flights Disc Golf, where he now serves as a trusted voice at the commentary desk.
