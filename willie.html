Willie the Monster

Bio

  • Name / Alias: Willie
  • Species: Monster (classification unknown)
  • Role: Chaos agent, comic relief
  • Disc Golf Strengths: Power, unpredictability, fearless lines
  • Disc Golf Weaknesses: Putting discipline, patience
  • Personality: Loud, energetic, mischievous, loyal
  • Connection to NFDG: Entertainment & hype character

Backstory

Willie the Monster doesn’t follow lines — he invents them. Born somewhere between a missed mando and a lucky kick, Willie thrives in chaos and feeds off crowd energy.

While others fear OB, Willie charges straight through it, laughing the whole way. He’s not here to win clean — he’s here to make the round unforgettable.
