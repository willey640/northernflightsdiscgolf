Our Governance Structure

At Northern Flights Disc Golf Inc., we are committed to transparency, accountability, and fostering a positive impact in the communities we serve. Our governance structure ensures that we operate with integrity, maintain community-centered values, and achieve our mission to grow disc golf and promote outdoor activities in Northern Canada.

Our Board of Directors

The Board of Directors is responsible for overseeing the strategic direction and ensuring the long-term sustainability of the organization. Our board consists of community leaders, disc golf enthusiasts, and individuals with diverse skills and backgrounds, all united by a shared passion for disc golf and community development.

Committees

To ensure that we are meeting the needs of the communities we serve, we have established the following committees:

1. Community and Outreach Committee

Focuses on building relationships with local communities, including Indigenous reservations, small towns, and Northern Canada. This committee also explores partnership opportunities to expand disc golf accessibility.

2. Programs and Clinics Committee

Responsible for organizing and running clinics, training, and other disc golf programs that promote outdoor activity, health, and fitness. The committee works closely with the community to offer tailored programs.

3. Finance and Grants Committee

Ensures the financial health of the organization through transparent budgeting and funding acquisition. This committee is dedicated to seeking grants and sponsorships to support our projects and initiatives.

4. Sponsorship and Partnerships Committee

Works to secure corporate and private sponsorships to support our athletes, events, and growth. This committee also fosters new partnerships with disc golf brands, local businesses, and organizations.

Code of Conduct

Our Board of Directors, staff, and volunteers are expected to uphold the highest ethical standards. We adhere to a strict Code of Conduct that promotes fairness, honesty, and respect in all interactions. Our commitment to the following principles helps ensure that Northern Flights Disc Golf Inc. remains a trusted and responsible organization:

  • Respect for all individuals: Embracing diversity and inclusion within the disc golf community and beyond.
  • Transparency: Open and clear communication with all stakeholders, including community members, sponsors, and partners.
  • Integrity: Operating in an ethical manner that reflects the values of our organization and the communities we serve.

Strategic Goals

Our long-term goals are centered around community impact, accessibility to the sport, and sustainable growth. As part of our governance, we regularly review and update our strategic plan to ensure we are on track to achieve our mission.

  • Creating disc golf courses: Designing and building accessible and enjoyable courses in small towns and Indigenous communities.
  • Sponsoring athletes: Supporting local talent and providing resources for disc golf enthusiasts to grow and compete at higher levels.
  • Running tournaments and clinics: Organizing events and educational programs to promote healthy living through disc golf.
