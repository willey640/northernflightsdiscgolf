1st Stop @ July 12, 2025 – Ski Hill Tournament

Northern Flights Disc Golf presents: the first stop of our Wellness Tour, happening Saturday, July 12 at the Flin Flon Ski Hill! This unique pop-up disc golf course takes players on a scenic hike with elevation changes, uphill and downhill shots, and beautiful views.

This free, all-ages event is designed to get our community outdoors and active. New players are welcome — if you've never played before, you can choose a free disc to keep!

Enjoy a free hot dog and drink (non-alcoholic), and enter to win prizes from our generous local sponsors: Creighton's Pizza, Domino's, The Rack, Gardewine, and Hudbay.

Registration is now open on UDisc!

View Our Ski Hill Caddy Book (PDF)

Please wear appropriate footwear, as the course winds through hilly, natural terrain. Bring your discs, your friends, and your best throws — it’s going to be a great day on the hill!

Tournament Update Flin Flon Ski Hill

June 14th's scheduled disc golf tournament at the Flin Flon Ski Hill has been cancelled due to the mandatory wildfire evacuation in the area.
At this time, we are unsure if the event can be rescheduled, but we will keep everyone updated as the situation develops.
Thank you for your understanding, and stay safe out there.
