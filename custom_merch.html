Artwork & File Guidelines

  • Best formats: SVG, PDF, AI (outlined), or high-res PNG (300+ DPI at print size).
  • Colors: Provide CMYK or RGB profiles; we’ll convert for sublimation.
  • Maps & Distances: Send hole lengths, par, OB/mandos, and a course map if available.
  • Logos: Vector preferred; raster logos may require cleanup.
  • Proofing: We send a digital proof for approval before production.

FAQ

Can you handle full 18- or 27-hole sign packages?

Yes. We can design, proof, and produce the entire set with consistent branding. Bulk pricing applies.

Do you design the hole maps?

We can draft maps from GPS files or sketches you provide and send a proof for approval.

What materials do you offer?

For signs: aluminum panels, high-grade plastics, or laminated composites; for tags: hardwoods, birch ply, or acrylic.
