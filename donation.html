Your Contribution Makes a Difference

Any donations big or small we take them all.

At Northern Flights Disc Golf Inc., we’re committed to promoting outdoor activities, healthy living, and community engagement across Northern Canada. Your support empowers us to:

  • Build and maintain accessible disc golf courses in small towns and Indigenous communities
  • Host tournaments and clinics to promote healthy living
  • Sponsor local athletes and emerging talent
  • Enhance outdoor recreational opportunities for all Canadians

How You Can Donate

  • One-Time Donation: Support our efforts with a single contribution
  • Monthly Donation: Offer steady support to fuel long-term growth
  • Corporate Sponsorship: Partner with us to make a lasting impact

You can e-transfer us at etransfer@northernflightsdiscgolf.ca
