Your Contribution Makes a Difference

At Northern Flights Disc Golf Inc., we are committed to promoting outdoor activities, healthy living, and community engagement by building disc golf courses across Northern Canada. With your support, we can expand our reach and empower communities through disc golf.

Your generous donation helps us to:

  • Build and maintain accessible disc golf courses in small towns and Indigenous communities
  • Host tournaments and clinics to promote healthy living
  • Sponsor local athletes and emerging talent
  • Enhance outdoor recreational opportunities for all Canadians

How You Can Donate

We offer several ways for you to contribute to our mission:

  • One-Time Donation: Make a one-time contribution to support our ongoing efforts.
  • Monthly Donation: Set up a recurring donation to provide continuous support for our programs and growth.
  • Corporate Sponsorship: Partner with us to sponsor events, courses, or athletes. Reach out to us for more details.
