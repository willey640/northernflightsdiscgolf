We follow official PDGA rules with a few regional tweaks to support our community:

  • Group Sizes: Minimum of 3 players per card, max of 5.
  • Casual Relief: Extended to 3 meters from standing water and thick brush.

  • Foot Faults: Warning on first offense, penalty begins on second.
