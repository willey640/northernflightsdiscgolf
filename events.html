About the Series

The Wellness Disc Golf Series is a traveling community initiative led by Northern Flights Disc Golf Inc. Our mission is to bring free, inclusive disc golf events to communities across Northern Saskatchewan, promoting physical activity, outdoor recreation, and mental wellness.
