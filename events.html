Bag Tags

  • Bag tags bring excitement, community, and friendly competition to disc golf! Whether you're playing casually or chasing the #1 tag, they add a fun layer of strategy to every round. Bag tags encourage regular play, connect players of all skill levels, and make each match feel meaningful. They’re a simple, affordable way to build camaraderie and keep the game engaging all season long.
