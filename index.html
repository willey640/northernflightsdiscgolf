Winter Creator Program ❄️

Winter’s coming. If you want a fun off-season project? We’re inviting content creators to help build the Northern Flights Disc Golf universe. We’ll issue a no-fee, non-exclusive license to use our images, logos, and characters (e.g., Bogey Man, Barlo, Willie, Ned, 90's good luck Troll) in approved projects that align with our goal of growing disc golf.

What you can make

  • Short videos & reels (course tips, trick shots, skits)
  • Commentary, voiceovers, jingles, and theme songs
  • Posters, memes, photo sets, and character art
  • Tutorials, gear spotlights, and wellness content

What you get

  • Official content license during the program
  • Social shout-outs and cross-posting
  • Chance for merch features & collabs
  • Eligibility for future paid projects (when available)

How to apply

  1. Send us links to your work and a short concept pitch.
  2. Tell us which characters/assets you’d like to use.
  3. Agree to our alignment & brand guidelines.
Apply Now
View Brand & Licensing Basics (PDF)

Licensing is valid for approved projects for the duration of the program and remains in effect as long as the content supports our mission to increase participation in disc golf. We reserve the right to revoke licensing for off-brand or harmful content.

June 30, 2025

Our Mission Statement

Our mission is to ignite a passion for disc golf across Northern Canada by building accessible and sustainable courses, sponsoring emerging talent, and hosting dynamic tournaments. We strive to create inclusive recreational opportunities that bring communities together, promote healthy lifestyles, and celebrate the beauty of the outdoors.
