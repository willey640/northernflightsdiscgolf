Our Mission

Our mission is to ignite a passion for disc golf across Northern Canada by building accessible and sustainable courses, sponsoring emerging talent, and hosting dynamic tournaments. We strive to create inclusive recreational opportunities that bring communities together, promote healthy lifestyles, and celebrate the beauty of the outdoors.

Our First Board Meeting

We’re thrilled to announce that Northern Flights Disc Golf Inc. held its first board meeting on April 17, 2025. The board discussed our vision for the future, and we’re excited to share the progress we’ve made. Here are some key highlights from the meeting:

  • Course Development: We’re working on plans for new courses in Denare Beach/ Crieghton Sk.
  • Community Engagement: Our upcoming spring league in Flin Flon, MB will be our first event .
  • Fundraising Efforts: We discussed ways to raise funds for future projects, including hosting tournaments and partnering with local businesses and applying for grants.

We are excited for the future and can't wait to continue working together to grow the sport of disc golf!
