The Mental Game of Disc Golf

Disc golf isn’t only about your arm speed or putting form—it’s a mental battle as much as a physical one. Players who invest in learning strategy, mindset, and course management often gain the edge needed to win close matches. That’s why we recommend resources that sharpen both your knowledge and your mental approach to the game.

September 29, 2025

Book 1: Zen and the Art of Disc Golf

Patrick McCormick’s Zen and the Art of Disc Golf is a modern classic that focuses on the mental side of the sport. Instead of worrying only about mechanics, McCormick teaches players to embrace mindfulness, focus, and a balanced mindset on the course. This book helps you approach disc golf with patience, confidence, and calm—transforming frustration into growth.

Buy on Amazon

Book 2: Disc Golf Strategy & Tactics

Disc Golf Strategy & Tactics: A Guide to Winning Disc Golf is a practical manual for improving your game. It breaks down everything from shot selection to mental preparation, helping players develop smarter approaches on the course. With diagrams and detailed explanations, this book is a must-read for anyone serious about lowering scores and playing more consistently.
