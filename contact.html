Interested in volunteering or joining the board? Let us know in your message!

Volunteering with Northern Flights Disc Golf is a chance to be part of something truly meaningful. Bringing accessible, outdoor recreation to communities across Northern Canada. Whether you're helping set up courses, run events, or support youth clinics, your time directly contributes to healthier lifestyles, community connection, and growing the sport of disc golf in remote and underserved areas. As a volunteer, you'll meet passionate people, gain hands-on experience, and make a lasting impact while having fun in the great outdoors. Join us in shaping a brighter, more active future for the North!
