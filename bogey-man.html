Bogey Man

Bio

  • Name / Alias: The Bogey Man
  • Species: Unknown
  • Role: Course menace, rules breaker, comic villain
  • Disc Golf Strengths: Chaos, intimidation, luck
  • Disc Golf Weaknesses: Consistency, sportsmanship
  • Personality: Loud, mischievous, competitive
  • Connection to NFDG: Antagonist character

Backstory

The Bogey Man appears whenever a round starts going wrong. Missed putts, bad kicks, foot faults — some say he causes them.

Whether myth or menace, players whisper his name after every unexpected bogey.
