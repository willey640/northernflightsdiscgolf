About the Series

The Flight School Series is a traveling community initiative led by Northern Flights Disc Golf Inc. Our mission is to bring free, inclusive disc golf events to communities across Northern Saskatchewan, promoting physical activity, outdoor recreation, and mental wellness.

Goals & Outcomes

  • Promote physical and mental wellness in rural communities
  • Encourage youth and family participation in outdoor recreation
  • Provide access to equipment and instruction at no cost
  • Build sustainable interest in disc golf and active living
