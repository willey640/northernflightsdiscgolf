June 7th NADGT Canada Exclusive: Devon Park - Presented by Innova

Get ready for an exciting day of disc golf action in The Pas, Manitoba! The NADGT Canada Exclusive: Devon Park - Presented by Innova is set for Saturday, June 7, 2025, bringing top amateur players together for a PDGA C-tier competition. Hosted by the National Amateur Disc Golf Tour, this tournament is part of the NADGT 2025 Season and promises a fantastic experience for players looking to compete, improve their game, and have fun. Whether you're chasing points, prizes, or just a great day on the course, Devon Park will be the place to be!
