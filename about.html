Our Mission

At Northern Flights Disc Golf Inc., our mission is to ignite a passion for disc golf across Northern Canada. We are committed to building accessible and sustainable disc golf courses, sponsoring emerging talent, and hosting dynamic tournaments. Our goal is to foster inclusive recreational opportunities that bring communities together, promote healthy lifestyles, and celebrate the beauty of the outdoors.

Our Vision

We envision a future where disc golf is accessible and enjoyed by communities across Northern Canada. By building courses in underserved areas, providing equipment and training, and hosting inclusive events, we hope to inspire a new generation of disc golfers in Indigenous and rural communities.

Get Involved

Whether you're a seasoned player or a total beginner there's a place for you. Donate, volunteer, or just spread the word. Every action helps us grow the sport and strengthen communities.
