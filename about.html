Our Mission

At **Northern Flights Disc Golf Inc.**, our mission is to ignite a passion for disc golf across Northern Canada. We are committed to building accessible and sustainable disc golf courses, sponsoring emerging talent, and hosting dynamic tournaments. Our goal is to foster inclusive recreational opportunities that bring communities together, promote healthy lifestyles, and celebrate the beauty of the outdoors.

Our Vision

We envision a future where disc golf is accessible and enjoyed by communities across Northern Canada. By building courses in underserved areas, providing equipment and training, and hosting inclusive events, we hope to inspire a new generation of disc golfers. Our work is centered around supporting Indigenous communities, small towns, and Northern regions where access to recreational activities can be limited.

Get Involved

There are many ways you can get involved with Northern Flights Disc Golf Inc. Whether you are an experienced disc golfer or new to the sport, we encourage you to support our mission through donations, volunteering, or simply spreading the word about our initiatives. Together, we can help grow the sport and create a lasting impact in Northern Canada.
